Salt

Salt is a mineral substance made of sodium chloride (NaCl), a chemical compound.

Salt is one of the oldest methods of food preservation. Salt is not only critical to your life, but it is one of the basic elements of which your body is made.

Salt has been used and traded for thousands of years.

The word salt originates from Latin. Salarium referred to the money paid to the Roman Army's soldiers for the purchase of salt. Today we still refer to one's pay as salary.
